    A friend for live

    Dogs are well known as the human most loyal companions. Many times they are our save gard, our friend or even we can see them as our little children.

    A dog can be a wonderful addition to any home, with or without children.

    So, if you are thinking about having a litle friend, you may consider adopting a dog.

    Adopt me!

    Adopting a dog will improve your life. Not only having a dog will keep you active but it will help you to reduces stress and it helps children to avoid allergies.

    8 reasons to adopt

    1. Because you'll save a life.

    2. Because you'll get a great animal.

    3. Because it'll cost you less.

    4. Because it's one way to fight puppy mills.

    5. Because your home will thanks you.

    6. Because all pets are good for your health, but adoptees offer an extra bosst.

    7. Because adoption helps more than just one animal.

    8. Because you'll change a homeless animal's whole world.

    Our response

    Some of them come from abusive houses. We also have some cases, where the owner cannot continue keeping it and taking care of him/her. We are motivated to save them from the streets.

    So if you see a dog that needs help. Do not doubt to contact us.

    Therapy program

    Mistreatment of animals ends leaving sequels in the dog that often are difficult to delete.

    However, we always do all is in our hand to make them feel safe and loveable.

    We treat them, and in very rare ocasions is necessary to re-educate them.

    To have more information about the programs we have, please contact us.
