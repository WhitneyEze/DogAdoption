    About us

    We are an organization (ONG) who cares about the treatment of all kind of animal, but especially dogs.

    We take care of them, feed them and we have a therapy program where they can be re-educated if it is necessary.

    Every case is different and every dog is special. So, if you are considering having a dog, please first consider adopting one of these dogs,who will give you a lot of love and he will be your best companion.
